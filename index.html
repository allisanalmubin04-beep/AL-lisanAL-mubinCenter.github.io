Center Policies

1. Cancellation Policy - سياسة الإلغاء
Student: Must notify teacher 3 hours before the scheduled lesson. Failure to notify will result in lesson fee deduction.
Teacher: Must notify student 3 hours before the scheduled lesson. Alternative time will be arranged for cancelled lessons.
2. Homework & Tasks Policy - سياسة الواجبات والمهام
Teacher Duties: Prepare lessons, provide required study materials, and give regular feedback to students.
Student Duties: Attend lessons regularly, complete homework assignments, and participate actively in lessons.
3. Attendance Policy - سياسة الحضور والانصراف
Attendance: Students must attend lessons at the scheduled time.
Late Arrival: If student is late, the delay time will be deducted from the lesson duration.
